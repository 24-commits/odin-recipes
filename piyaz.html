Piyaz

Description

Piyaz is a super fresh dish that you can have as a side or main dish.

It's perfect to have during summer as you can have it cold.

Ingredients

  • Witte bonen - soaked in water 8h-12h prior to cooking
  • Rode ui (optional but definitely recommended)
  • Groene peper
  • Tomaat
  • Parsley
  • Azijn
  • Olijf olie
  • Citroen
  • Sumak
  • Zout

Steps

  1. Boil the beans
  2. Cut the red onion halve maand
  3. Cut the green pepper
  4. Dice the tomaat
  5. Dice the parsley
  6. Mix all the cut vegetables with the beans in a bowl
  7. Add azijn, citroen, olijf olie, sumak en zout & mix again
