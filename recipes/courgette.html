Courgette Recipe

Description

Courgette is a perfect dish that you can have warm or cold.

Ingredients

  • 2x Courgette
  • Ui
  • 3 of 4 tomaten
  • tomaten pasta
  • 1 wortel
  • Olijf olie
  • Zout

Steps

  1. Dice the onion
  2. Add olie on the pan, once it's warm enough add the onions.
  3. Meanwhile cut the wortel.
  4. Add the wortel. While it's getting wat gaar cut the courgette and tomatoes.
  5. Add the tomaten pasta, stir
  6. Add courgette and tomaten. stir a bit and add some salt and then put the lit on and leave it for about 30 mins.
  7. It should be done. No, there is no need to add any water!
